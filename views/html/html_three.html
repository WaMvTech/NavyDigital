    Paragraph Tags

    The reason for this is the way HTML is eventually parsed by the browser. With no tags separating the two lines, the content is essentially treated as being together, and are thus rendered together, on the same line. To get the desired effect in this case, you will need to use the paragraph tag, or p tag.

    There we go! With each line being encompassed by their own paragraph tag, the browser treats them as separate pieces of content and renders them as such.
