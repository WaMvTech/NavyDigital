    Unordered Lists

    Like the names imply, the only difference between an ordered list and an unordered list, is that an ordered list will have its items ordered by number, whereas unordered lists will have just bullet points. Making your own unordered list is straightforward. You just wrap an unordered list tag, or ul tag, around your list items, which use li tags.
